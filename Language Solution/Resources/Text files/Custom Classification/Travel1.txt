Eco-Tourism:

Eco-tourism is gaining traction among travelers who seek to minimize their environmental impact while exploring new destinations. This sustainable approach to travel emphasizes the conservation of nature, support for local communities, and education about the local environment. Eco-tourists often engage in activities like wildlife watching, hiking in protected areas, and staying in eco-friendly accommodations that use renewable energy sources, reduce waste, and promote recycling. By choosing eco-tourism, travelers play a pivotal role in preserving the natural and cultural heritage of the destinations they visit.
