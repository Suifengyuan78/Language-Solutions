Adventure Travel: 

Adventure travel caters to those seeking an adrenaline rush and a break from the conventional sightseeing tours. This type of travel involves exploring remote areas, engaging in physical activities, and embracing the great outdoors. Whether it's white-water rafting, mountain climbing, safari trekking, or scuba diving, adventure travel offers an exhilarating way to experience the world. It not only challenges the traveler physically and mentally but also provides unique opportunities to interact with nature and learn about oneself.
